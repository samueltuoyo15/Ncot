pillars of islam

Shahada

(Faith)

The declaration, "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His Messenger."

Salah

(Prayer)

Performing five daily prayers at prescribed times.

Zakat

(Charity)

Giving 2.5% of one’s savings to the needy annually.

Sawm

(Fasting)

Fasting from dawn to sunset during the month of Ramadan.

Hajj

(Pilgrimage)

Making the pilgrimage to Mecca at least once in a lifetime, if able.
